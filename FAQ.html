Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I sign up for Chirp?

To sign up for Chirp, click on the "Sign in with Twitter" button and follow the instructions to authorize Chirp to access your Twitter account data.

2. Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free trial that allows you to try out Chirp's features for a limited time.

3. Can I schedule tweets for multiple accounts?

Currently, Chirp supports scheduling tweets for a single Twitter account per user.

4. How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription by going to your account settings and selecting the "Cancel Subscription" option.

5. Are there any limitations on the number of tweets I can schedule?

With the basic subscription, you can schedule up to 50 tweets per month. If you need more, consider upgrading to a higher plan.
